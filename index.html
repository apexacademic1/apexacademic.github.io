FAQs

Questions parents ask before booking

How do I know which tutor my student will get?

After you submit the intake form, we review your student's needs and match them with the best-fit tutor. You receive an intro email with your tutor's name, credentials, and relevant background before the first session.

Can I switch tutors if it's not a good fit?

Yes, always. Just contact us and we'll arrange a new match, no awkward conversations required. Your sessions carry over.

What platform do sessions happen on?

Sessions are held online via Zoom or Google Meet, with a shared digital whiteboard. All your student needs is a device with a camera and internet.

How do session notes work?

Within 2 hours of every session, your tutor submits a structured note covering what was taught, what your student still needs to practice, and what's planned for next time. We email it directly to you.

Do packages expire?

Packages are valid for 90 days from the date of purchase. If you need an extension, contact us we handle it case by case.

What if my student has a learning difference?

Let us know on your intake form. We ask about accommodations and match students with tutors who have relevant experience. We currently serve students with ADHD, dyslexia, and other common learning differences.

How quickly can we start?

Most families book their first session within 48–72 hours of submitting an intake form. If you need something sooner, note it in your form and we'll prioritize your match.

What grades and ages do you serve?

We currently serve students in grades 7–12 (ages approximately 12–18). Our tutors specialize in middle and high school curriculum and college prep.

About

A Tutoring Company Built on Standards

Apex Academic was built on a simple belief: students perform better when they have consistent, accountable support and parents should never have to wonder whether their investment is working.

We are a managed tutoring company, which means we do more than connect you with a tutor. We screen and interview every tutor on our roster, match students based on subject, schedule, and learning style, and send parents a written session update after every lesson.

We work with families across the United States, serving students in grades 7–12 across math, science, English, and test preparation. Our focus is high school, because that's where access to the right support matters most for a student's future options.

Why we built this differently

Most tutoring platforms are marketplaces you browse profiles, read reviews, pick someone, and hope. There's no matching, no quality control, and no accountability after the session ends. You don't know what happened during the lesson or whether it helped.

Apex Academic is different because we treat tutoring as a managed service, not a gig economy platform. We hire tutors the way a company hires staff with interviews, assessments, and standards. We assign them the way a company assigns work based on fit, not availability. And we hold them accountable the way a company manages performance with documented standards and monthly reviews.

About the founder

Apex Academic was founded by Isti Khalil, a Behavioral Neuroscience student at Northeastern University with three years of hands-on research experience at the Wistar Institute and the University of Pennsylvania Perelman School of Medicine. He has seen firsthand how much access to quality academic support shapes a student's trajectory, and he built Apex Academic to make that access reliable, consistent, and results-driven.

As founder and CEO, Isti oversees tutor recruitment, quality control, and all operations so every family gets a consistent, professional experience from day one.
